About me

Expert in Feng Shui for businesses, houses and people

I have many years involved in the study and practice of metaphysics, and feng shui , I have participated as a student in different schools and workshops in a formal way or for my own interest here in Mexico. I am always up to date with the latest news about it . In the last 15 years I have worked for interior designers and decoration companies , art galleries and also holistic medicine. Currently I have put into practice my knowledge and I was focused on helping people in the healing and redisigning of their spaces , businesses, homes and environments. Currently I participate in radio programs in Los Cabos, I give talks and workshops to improve the quality of life through Feng Shui.

I consider that our personal space is the most important center where our life begins, it is the place were we connect with our inner soul and it is the reflection to the outside.

That's where your true desires, your inspiration, your breath, your creations and prayers are born. Decoration is important to give a good specific image to any space, be it business, home, park, meditation room etc ..., but it is more important how you manage the elements and energies of the place because if there is no friendly atmosphere it is difficult that you can manifest something prosperous in any aspect of your life.

I can feel beyond the appearances of the images and know how things go in their places, sometimes people use tetric, dark, lugubrious colors and that is what they attract to their life, pure shadows ........ there are also people that like to accumalate things and the only thing they do is block the flow of energy and nothing happens there ...................... people feel that the world is coming on top of them and they can not move forward, they leave everything for latter.

You must be in contact with nature to refresh your energies, this is the purpose to bring nature into your space.

It is necessary to harmonize and heal our environments and our person in order to let good energies flow, putting our atmosphere in peace and order with a lot of love and affection.
